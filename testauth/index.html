Welcome back!

All right, so now we're going to bounce from here to Imgur, passing your application's clientId so Imgur knows what app is trying to access the currently-logged-in account.

You have a clientId, right? You got it when you registered your application with Imgur. You received an email with it and the clientSecret. If you haven't done that yet, go do that now and come back here. When you register, select Authorization type "OAuth 2 authorization with a callback URL" and-- for now-- enter http://localhost:54321/gotcode as the Authorization callback URL.

If you have already registered your app, make sure the callback URL is set as above. You can do this in the "applications" section of your Imgur Account Settings.

Now to make a request to Imgur to get an authorization code, we need to send your application's clientId (NOT the clientSecret).
